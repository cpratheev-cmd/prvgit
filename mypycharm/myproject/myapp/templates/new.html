About the System

Smart Student Dropout Prediction System

The Smart Student Dropout Prediction System is designed to help educational institutions identify students who are at risk of dropping out at an early stage. By analyzing key academic indicators such as attendance, marks, assignments, and classroom participation, the system predicts potential dropout risks.

This platform enables teachers and administrators to take proactive measures, provide academic support, and improve student retention. By using data-driven insights, institutions can create a more supportive learning environment for students.

  • 📊 Data-driven student analysis
  • 🎯 Early risk detection
  • 📧 Automated alerts and notifications
  • 👨‍🏫 Helps teachers support students effectively

Key Features

✅Weighted Scoring

Calculates a risk score using attendance, marks, assignments, and participation.

📱Instant Risk Detection

Automatically evaluates and categorizes students into Low, Medium, or High risk.

🚨Alerts & 🔔Notifications

Sends email alerts to parents and faculty when a student is at high risk.
